Air Fried Hamburger Patties

Description:

An easy western dish that any student can cook and enjoy. Takes less than 30 minutes prepare and make with enough to fill for the evening.

Ingrediants:

  • 1 pound 80% lean ground beef
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon beef base
  • freshly ground black pepper

Cooking Instructions:

  1. Prep phase:
    1. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F.
    2. Form beef into 4 patties.
    3. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boiling temperture.
  2. Whisk together warm melted butter and beef soup base in a small bowl.
    • Apply to both sides of the patties.
  3. For Medium:
    • Leave in for 7 minutes.
    • Flip at 3 and a half minutes.
  4. For Well-done:
    • Leave in for 9 minutes.
    • Flip at 4 and a half minutes.
